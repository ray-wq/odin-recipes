Pink Lemonade

Description

Refreshing pink lemonade for the summertime

Ingredients

  • Lemon juice
  • Granulated sugar
  • Cranberry juice
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Adjust the recipe to suit your taste. You can easily add more sugar or lemon juice to make it more sweet or tart.
  2. Store in the refrigerator, and serve over ice.
  3. If you notice any separation after chilling, give the lemonade a stir right before serving
